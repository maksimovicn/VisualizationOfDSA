Visualisation of DSA Algorithm is software developed to help 
visualize the digital signature algorithm developed by 
the NIST (National Institute Of Technology) as a part of 
DSS (Digital Signature Standard). The software is 
developed at School of Electrical Engineering of 
University of Belgrade, Serbia as a thesis of student
Nikola Maksimovic, supervised by prof Zarko Stanisavljevic.
